Ophthalmology Department

Dedicated to preserving and restoring vision through comprehensive medical and surgical eye care.

Advanced Care for Your Vision

The Ophthalmology Department at MediCare Hospital offers a complete range of services for the health of your eyes. Our team of ophthalmologists, optometrists, and technicians are committed to providing the highest standard of eye care for patients of all ages.

We use the latest diagnostic technology and treatment techniques to manage everything from routine eye exams to complex eye diseases and surgeries. Your vision is our priority, and we are dedicated to helping you see your world clearly.

Our Ophthalmology Services

We provide a full spectrum of diagnostic, medical, and surgical eye care services.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Routine check-ups, vision testing, and screening for common eye diseases.

Cataract Surgery

Advanced, minimally invasive procedures to restore clear vision from cataracts.

Glaucoma Management

Early diagnosis and personalized treatment plans to preserve vision from glaucoma.

Retina Services

Expert care for conditions like diabetic retinopathy and macular degeneration.

Cornea & External Disease

Treatment for corneal infections, dystrophies, and other surface eye issues.

Pediatric Ophthalmology

Specialized eye care for infants, children, and adolescents.
