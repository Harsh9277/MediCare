Maternity Care

Supporting you through every step of your pregnancy journey with expert, compassionate, and personalized care.

Your Partner in Pregnancy & Childbirth

Welcome to the Maternity Center at MediCare Hospital, where we celebrate the miracle of birth. Our dedicated team of obstetricians, midwives, nurses, and lactation consultants is here to provide comprehensive care for you and your baby in a safe, comfortable, and family-centered environment.

We believe in empowering mothers with knowledge and choices, offering personalized birth plans and a range of support services. From your first prenatal visit to the moment you take your baby home, we are honored to be a part of your special journey.

Our Maternity Services

We offer a full spectrum of services to ensure a healthy pregnancy, safe delivery, and happy beginning for your family.

Prenatal Care

Comprehensive checkups, advanced ultrasound imaging, and genetic screening.

Labor & Delivery Suites

Private, comfortable birthing suites with advanced monitoring and pain management options.

High-Risk Pregnancy

Specialized care from maternal-fetal medicine experts for complex pregnancies.

Neonatal ICU (NICU)

Level III NICU with expert neonatologists for premature or critically ill newborns.

Lactation Support

Certified lactation consultants to assist with breastfeeding education and support.

Postpartum Care

Dedicated support for mothers' physical and emotional recovery after childbirth.
