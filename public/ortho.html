Orthopedics Department

Comprehensive care for bones, joints, ligaments, and muscles to help you move without pain and live life to the fullest.

Advanced Orthopedic & Sports Medicine

The Orthopedics Department at MediCare Hospital provides a full range of services for musculoskeletal conditions. Our team of board-certified orthopedic surgeons, physical therapists, and sports medicine specialists is committed to restoring function and relieving pain for our patients.

We utilize the latest minimally invasive surgical techniques, cutting-edge diagnostics, and evidence-based rehabilitation protocols to ensure the best possible outcomes. From total joint replacement to complex fracture care, we are here to get you back on your feet.

Our Orthopedic Specialties

We provide specialized care across all areas of orthopedics to meet your unique needs.

Joint Replacement

Minimally invasive hip, knee, and shoulder replacement surgery for arthritis and injury.

Sports Medicine

Comprehensive care for athletes, including ACL reconstruction and rotator cuff repair.

Fracture & Trauma Care

Expert management of all types of bone fractures and musculoskeletal trauma.

Hand & Upper Extremity

Specialized treatment for conditions affecting the hand, wrist, elbow, and shoulder.

Foot & Ankle Surgery

Advanced care for bunions, ankle instability, Achilles tendon injuries, and more.

Physical Therapy

Personalized rehabilitation programs to restore strength, mobility, and function.
