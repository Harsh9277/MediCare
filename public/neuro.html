Neurology Department

Expert diagnosis and treatment for the full spectrum of neurological disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

Comprehensive Neurological Care

The Neurology Department at MediCare Hospital is dedicated to providing exceptional care for patients with disorders of the nervous system. Our team of highly skilled neurologists, neurosurgeons, and support staff utilize a patient-centered approach to manage and treat a wide variety of conditions.

We combine clinical expertise with the latest diagnostic technologies and advanced treatment modalities to create personalized care plans. From common conditions like migraines to complex cases like stroke and epilepsy, our goal is to improve your quality of life.

Our Neurology Services

We offer a wide range of specialized services for various neurological conditions.

Stroke Management

Rapid "code stroke" response team and comprehensive post-stroke rehabilitation services.

Epilepsy & Seizure Care

Advanced diagnostics, long-term monitoring, and personalized treatment plans.

Headache & Migraine Clinic

Specialized diagnosis and innovative treatments for all types of chronic headaches.

Movement Disorders

Expert care for Parkinson's disease, tremors, dystonia, and other movement disorders.

Neurophysiology Lab

State-of-the-art EEG, EMG, and nerve conduction studies for accurate diagnosis.

Memory Disorders

Compassionate evaluation and management of Alzheimer's, dementia, and memory loss.
