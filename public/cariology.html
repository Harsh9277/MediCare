Cardiology Department

Delivering expert heart care with advanced technology and a compassionate touch. Your cardiovascular health is our priority.

Leading the Way in Heart Health

The MediCare Hospital Cardiology Department is a center of excellence, offering comprehensive care for a wide spectrum of cardiovascular diseases. Our team of renowned cardiologists, surgeons, and dedicated nurses work together to provide personalized treatment plans for each patient.

From preventative care and diagnostics to complex interventional procedures and rehabilitation, we utilize the latest medical advancements to ensure the best possible outcomes for your heart health.

Our Cardiology Services

We provide a full range of services to diagnose, treat, and manage heart and vascular conditions.

Advanced Diagnostics

Utilizing ECG, Echocardiography, Stress Tests, and Holter monitoring for precise diagnosis.

Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive procedures including angioplasty, stenting, and TAVI.

Electrophysiology

Treatment for heart rhythm disorders (arrhythmias), including pacemakers and ablation.

Preventive Care

Focused programs on risk assessment, lifestyle modification, and managing cholesterol.

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Customized programs to help patients recover quickly after a cardiac event or surgery.

Cardiac Imaging

State-of-the-art cardiac CT and MRI for detailed views of the heart's structure and function.
