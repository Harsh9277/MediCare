Advanced Surgical Solutions

The Surgery Department at MediCare Hospital provides a full range of surgical services. Our board-certified surgeons are leaders in their fields, equipped with state-of-the-art operating theaters and advanced robotic and minimally invasive technologies.

We are committed to providing the highest standard of surgical care, focusing on patient safety, optimal outcomes, and a compassionate patient experience. From routine procedures to complex operations, our team is here for you.

Our Surgical Specialties

We offer a wide range of specialized surgical procedures and techniques.

General Surgery

Comprehensive care for abdominal issues, including hernia repair and appendectomies.

Cardiothoracic Surgery

Advanced procedures for the heart, lungs, and chest, including bypass surgery.

Orthopedic Surgery

Specializing in joint replacements, sports injuries, and complex fracture care.

Neurosurgery

Expert surgical treatment for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system.

Minimally Invasive Surgery

Laparoscopic and endoscopic techniques for faster recovery and less scarring.

Robotic-Assisted Surgery

Utilizing the latest robotic technology for enhanced precision in complex operations.
