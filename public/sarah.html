About Dr. Sarah Johnson

Dr. Sarah Johnson is a board-certified cardiologist at MediCare Hospital with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing and treating a wide range of cardiovascular diseases. She is deeply committed to providing patient-centered care, combining clinical expertise with a compassionate and empathetic approach.

Her philosophy is centered on preventative care and patient education, empowering individuals to take an active role in their heart health. Dr. Johnson believes in building lasting relationships with her patients, ensuring they feel heard, understood, and confident in their treatment plans.

Education & Qualifications

  • Fellowship in Interventional Cardiology: Cleveland Clinic, Cleveland, OH
  • Residency in Internal Medicine: Johns Hopkins Hospital, Baltimore, MD
  • Doctor of Medicine (M.D.): Stanford University School of Medicine, Stanford, CA
  • Board Certified: American Board of Internal Medicine (Cardiovascular Disease)

Areas of Expertise

  • Interventional Cardiology (Stents and Angioplasty)
  • Preventive Cardiology and Risk Factor Management
  • Advanced Echocardiography and Cardiac Imaging
  • Heart Failure Management
  • Treatment of Arrhythmias
