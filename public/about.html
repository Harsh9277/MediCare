About MediCare Hospital

Committed to providing compassionate and innovative healthcare solutions for a healthier community.

Our Story and Philosophy

Established in 1998, MediCare Hospital has grown to become a beacon of medical excellence. Our mission and vision guide everything we do.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, patient-centered healthcare with compassion, integrity, and respect. We are dedicated to advancing medical knowledge, delivering outstanding service, and improving the health of the diverse communities we serve.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider in the region, recognized for our clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and unwavering commitment to the well-being of our patients and their families.

Our Core Values

Our values are the foundation of our hospital's culture and guide our interactions with patients and colleagues alike.

Dedicated to Excellence in Care

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our practice, from the latest medical technology to our highly-trained staff. We continuously strive to set new standards in healthcare.

Compassion

Treating every patient with empathy, kindness, and respect.

Innovation

Embracing new technologies and treatments to improve patient outcomes.

Collaboration

Working together as a team to provide holistic and integrated care.

Our Hospital's Journey

A look back at the key milestones that shaped MediCare Hospital into the institution it is today.

1998 - Founding

MediCare Hospital was founded with a vision for advanced and accessible community healthcare.

2005 - New Wing Opens

We expanded our facilities by adding a new wing dedicated to cardiology and neurology departments.

2012 - Certified Trauma Center

Received certification as a Level II Trauma Center, equipped to handle critical medical emergencies.

2020 - Telemedicine Launch

Launched our telemedicine platform to connect patients with specialists from anywhere.
