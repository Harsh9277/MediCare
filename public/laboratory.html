Laboratory Services

Accurate, reliable, and timely diagnostic testing from our state-of-the-art clinical laboratory.

Advanced Clinical Testing

The Clinical Laboratory at MediCare Hospital plays a crucial role in patient care. Our team of certified medical technologists and pathologists uses automated, state-of-the-art equipment to perform a wide range of diagnostic tests.

We are committed to providing precise and prompt results, which are vital for accurate diagnosis, treatment planning, and monitoring of diseases. Our lab adheres to the highest standards of quality control and safety for all procedures.

Our Key Testing Areas

We provide a comprehensive menu of tests to support all medical specialties.

Hematology

Analysis of blood cells and coagulation to diagnose anemia, infections, and leukemia.

Clinical Chemistry

Measures chemical components in blood to monitor organ function and metabolic processes.

Microbiology

Identifies bacteria, viruses, and fungi to diagnose infections and guide antibiotic therapy.

Pathology

Microscopic examination of tissues and cells to diagnose cancer and other diseases from biopsies.

Immunology & Serology

Detects antibodies and antigens to diagnose autoimmune disorders and viral infections.

Urinalysis

Routine and microscopic examination of urine to detect kidney disease, diabetes, and UTIs.
