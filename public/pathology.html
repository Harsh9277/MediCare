Pathology Department

The science behind the cure. Providing accurate, reliable laboratory testing for definitive disease diagnosis.

Definitive Diagnostic Answers

The Pathology Department at MediCare Hospital is the cornerstone of diagnostic medicine. Our expert pathologists and laboratory scientists work behind the scenes to analyze tissue, blood, and fluid samples, providing the crucial information that guides patient care.

With a fully accredited, state-of-the-art laboratory, we deliver rapid and precise results. From routine blood tests to complex genetic analysis, our commitment to quality and accuracy ensures your healthcare team has the answers they need.

Our Laboratory Services

We provide a comprehensive range of diagnostic laboratory testing and analysis.

Histopathology

Microscopic examination of tissue samples to diagnose diseases like cancer.

Clinical Chemistry

Analysis of blood and body fluids for metabolic panels, enzyme levels, and more.

Hematology

Study of blood, blood-forming organs, and blood diseases like anemia and leukemia.

Molecular Diagnostics

Advanced testing of DNA and RNA for genetic disorders and personalized medicine.

Microbiology

Identification of bacteria, viruses, and fungi to diagnose and treat infections.

Cytopathology

Examination of individual cells, such as in Pap tests, to screen for disease.
